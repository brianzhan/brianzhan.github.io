Mediation Meditation
Welcome to Meditation Meditation. We aim to make this process as smooth as possible. For that to happen, we need the full cooperation of all parties involved throughout this process. Please take the time to gather all of the appropriate documents and information that you will need to answer questions about your finances for your most previous fiscal year as well as any assets that you own. We will walk you step-by-step through the entire process and give clear instruction on how to successfully complete this part of the process. If at any time you have any questions, please contact your mediator or financial advisor so that they can give you further instruction. Both parties will be completing the exact same process, but separately, to give a clear picture to the mediator and financial analyst what assets may be in contention, and what each parties financial knowledge is. These questions will ask you to provide information about your income and your property. When possible, use exact numbers provided by property appraisals. If you are unsure of the exact value of something, then use your best judgement to estimate the objects value.
